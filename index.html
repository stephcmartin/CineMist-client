About CineMist
You're at a bar and someone recommends you a movie that you should watch. "You'll love it", they said. You scribbe it down somewhere on a napkin, stick it in your pocket.

Two hours later, the napkin is nowhere to be found.

We created CineMist so that you no longer have to write your to-watch lists on sticky notes, whiteboards, random scraps of paper, or the back of your hand.
